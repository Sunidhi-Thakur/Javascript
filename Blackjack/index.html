Blackjack

About:

Equally well known as Twenty-One. The rules are simple, the play is thrilling, and there is opportunity for high strategy. In fact, for the expert player who mathematically plays a perfect game and is able to count cards, the odds are sometimes in that player's favor to win.

Object of the Game:

Each participant attempts to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.

Card Values:

An ace is worth 1 or 11 depending on your current score. Face cards are 10 and any other card is its pip value.

How to Play?

  1. Press 'Hit' to start your turn. Keep pressing hit unless desired score(<=21) is reached.
  2. Once your desired score is reached, press 'Stand' for computer's turn.
  3. After computer has played, winner will be decided.
  4. Press 'Deal' to start a New Game.
